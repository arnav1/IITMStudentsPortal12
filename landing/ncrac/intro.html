About IIT Madras

Indian Institute of Technology Madras is one of the premier institutions of national importance in higher technical education and basic & applied research. It was established in 1959 by the Government of India in collaboration with the German Government. IIT Madras is a township of about one square mile area located in the city of Chennai, previously known as Madras. It is a residential institution with about 510 faculty, 7800 students and 625 supporting staff. The Institute has sixteen academic departments and a few advanced research centers in various disciplines of engineering and pure sciences, with nearly 100 laboratories. The self-contained township carved out of the Guindy National Park with wild life houses nearly 15,000 residents. It has improved the flora and fauna over the years & has about 300 species of trees and plants including native wild ones. It is conveniently located at a distance of about 12 km each from airport and railway station.

About ISHRAE & ASHRAE

Indian Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air Conditioning Engineers (ISHRAE) was established in 1981 as a society of professionals and now it comprises 35 chapters spread all over India with over 9000 members. One of its objectives is to disseminate technical knowledge in the field of RAC through periodic seminars, lectures, conferences, and exhibitions (visit website www.ishrae.in).
American Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air-Conditioning Engineers (ASHRAE) - an international organization, aims to serve humanity and promote a sustainable world by disseminating knowledge in the areas of heating, ventilation, air conditioning and refrigeration (visit website www.ashrae.org).
ASHRAE Chennai Chapter, the second in South India was started in the year 2009 and works to accomplish and fulfill the goals of ASHRAE, USA. It conducts seminars, bi-monthly technical talks in various fields mentioned above.
